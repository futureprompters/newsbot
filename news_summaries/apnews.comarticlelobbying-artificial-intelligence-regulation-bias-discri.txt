**Title:** Bipartisan Efforts to Regulate AI Face Challenges  
**Summary:** Lawmakers in several states are working on proposals to address bias in AI decision-making, with labor unions and advocacy groups pushing for more transparency and legal recourse for citizens. The industry, while offering some support, is concerned about potential accountability measures. The proposed bills emphasize the need for oversight and impact assessments for AI systems, aiming to mitigate discrimination risks. However, there are debates over self-reporting by companies, public access to impact assessments, and who can file lawsuits under the legislation.
