**Title:** UK and South Korea to co-host AI Seoul Summit

**Summary:** The UK and South Korea are joining forces to co-host the AI Seoul Summit in May, focusing on the safe development of AI technologies. The summit aims to continue the discussions on AI safety, inclusion, and innovation, building upon the cooperative framework laid down by the Bletchley Declaration. Key figures from both countries will lead sessions to strengthen global cooperation on AI governance and innovation, reflecting the commitment to harnessing AI advancements for the benefit of humanity.
