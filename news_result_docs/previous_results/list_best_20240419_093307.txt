Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming healthcare by revolutionizing care delivery, operational efficiencies, and research acceleration. Despite challenges in AI adoption due to regulations and data requirements in healthcare, AI is already making significant improvements in key areas such as automating medical record reviews, identifying costly coverage errors, optimizing medication adherence, and harnessing the power of generative AI. The success of AI in healthcare depends on the quality of data and responsible implementation to ultimately enhance patient care outcomes.
